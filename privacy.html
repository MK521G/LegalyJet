This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. 1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you use our contact form, you may provide your name and email address so we can respond to your inquiry. We only use this information to reply to your message. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Document generation data

LegalyJet uses minimal cookies. We may set a session cookie to maintain your browser session and enable basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking cookies, or third-party marketing pixels. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

We use the limited information we collect to:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable law to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at our contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

LegalyJet is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
